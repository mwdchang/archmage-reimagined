## Kingdom 
Each turn you spend, your kingdom produces and accumulates resources.

You can check the game settings page to view the production rate for each building.


### Resources
There are three main resources

#### Mana
You need mana to cast spells, most units also have mana upkeep.

#### Geld
Geld is the currency in Terra, you use geld to bid on magical items, recruit units, and pay units upkeep.

#### Population
Population generate geld for your kingdom, and provide new recruits to your barracks.

### Buildings

#### Farms
Everyone eats, farms produce food for your population.

#### Towns
Towns provide living spaces for your population.

#### Workshops
More workshops increase your building rate

#### Barracks
Barracks allow you to train your special units.

#### Nodes
Nodes both produce and store mana. The more nodes you build the more storage room you have. Mana production is graded on a decreasing return scale. 

#### Guilds
Guilds generate research points (for learning spells) and skill points (for gaining skills).

#### Forts
Forts are the lifeblood of your kingdom. You are defeated if your forts are reduced to 0. 

#### Barriers
Barriers provide a chance to block battle and non-battle spells cast against your kingdom. Barriers max out at 2.5%
